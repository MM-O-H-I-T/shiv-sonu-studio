Frequently Asked Questions

Which formats do you convert?

Aap VHS, MiniDV, Hi8, Video8, CD aur DVD jaise formats ko high-quality MP4 mein convert karwa sakte hain.

How long does the conversion take?

Generally 2–5 din lagte hain, depending on tapes/discs ki quantity aur condition.

Does video quality improve?

Hum best possible quality maintain karte hain. Final output original tape ki condition par depend karta hai.

Can damaged or fungus tapes be recovered?

Haan, hum fungus cleaning aur tape repair (splicing) jaise methods use karte hain. Har case mein recovery possible nahi hoti, lekin maximum effort diya jata hai.

Is my original media safe?

Bilkul. Aapki tapes ko carefully handle kiya jata hai aur kaam ke baad safely return kiya jata hai.

What format will I receive?

Aapko video MP4 format mein milega, jo mobile, TV aur computer sab par easily chal jata hai.

How will I receive my videos?

Pendrive, Hard Drive ya Google Drive link — aap apni preference choose kar sakte hain.

Can I courier my tapes?

Haan, aap India ke kisi bhi kone se courier kar sakte hain. Receive hote hi WhatsApp confirmation milta hai.

What if my tape cannot be converted?

Agar recovery possible nahi hoti, toh hum aapki original tape aur pendrive bina kisi hidden charges ke safely return kar dete hain.

How are charges calculated?

Charges tape/disc type, duration aur condition (normal ya damaged) par depend karte hain. Aapko pehle free quote diya jata hai.
